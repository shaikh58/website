Mustafa Shaikh

I work with deep learning and robotics. I like to work on projects with a path to an end product that others can use.

I'm currently working on a multi-object tracking problem using deep learning, with Cell Biology at UC San Diego and Computational Neuroscience at SALK Institute for Biomedical Research.

During my graduate research, I worked on a trajectory optimization problem with a limited field of view in a pursuer-evader scenario. We demonstrated our method on a real robot.

Previously, I worked in industry for ~5 years in large organizations in a data science role. I worked closely with business stakeholders and led projects end to end.

I completed my graduate studies in Electrical and Computer Engineering at UC San Diego, and my undergraduate studies in Engineering Science at the University of Toronto.
